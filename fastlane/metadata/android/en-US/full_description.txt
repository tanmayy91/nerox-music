Nerox Music is a free, open-source, cross-platform music streaming app built with Flutter.
Stream millions of songs from YouTube and YouTube Music — no ads, no login, no limits.

Features:

• Stream music from YouTube / YouTube Music
• Background playback with system media controls
• Streaming quality control (Low / High / Best)
• Song caching while playing for instant replay
• Song download support for offline playback
• Synced and plain lyrics via LRCLIB
• Dynamic theme that adapts to album artwork
• Create and manage playlists
• Bookmark albums and artists
• Import songs, playlists, albums, and artists by sharing from YouTube Music
• Radio feature for endless music discovery
• Skip silence for seamless listening
• Equalizer support (Android)
• Android Auto support
• Sleep timer
• Piped playlist integration
• Flexible navigation (bottom bar or side rail)
• Responsive layout for phones, tablets, and desktops
• No advertisements
• No login required
• No tracking or data collection

Nerox Music is completely free and does not collect any personal data.
